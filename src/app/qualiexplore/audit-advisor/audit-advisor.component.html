Audit Advisor Assistant

Welcome to the Audit Advisor chatbot, brought to you by the i4Q project. You can access the chatbot by simply clicking the icon located on the lower right side of this page. Our chatbot is designed to assist you in preparing for quality audits with a specific focus on data quality.

Data quality refers to how well the specific characteristics of data align with stated requirements. Similar to product and process quality, data quality plays a crucial role in ensuring accurate and reliable information. Here are some key quality characteristics of data:

  • Accuracy: The extent to which data attributes correctly represent the true value of the intended attributes within a given context.
  • Precision: The degree to which data attributes are exact or provide discrimination within a specific context of use.
  • Credibility: The level of trustworthiness and believability that users associate with the attributes of data within a specific context.
  • Currentness: The relevance of data attributes based on their timeliness within a specific context of use.

To experience the capabilities of Audit Advisor, we encourage you to give it a try. Simply ask "What can you do?" to obtain an overview of the chatbot's functionalities.

Internally, the assistant uses an extensive multi-level questionnaire with more than 2000 specific questions developed by the Technical University of Berlin (TUB). BIBA translated this questionnaire into a knowledge graph that the chatbot can access. The chatbot uses Rasa Open Source, which significantly differs from recent generative models. It is more reliable and does not hallucinate but at the expense of being less flexible and human-like in its responses.

This is a prototypical implementation with some constraints:

  • All users share the same projects
  • Higher probability that a conversation results in an out-of-scope response like "Sorry, I cannot help you."
  • Higher probability the bot respondes with irrational answers in longer conversations
  • Errors in the underlying questionnaire, such as missing descriptions and typos
