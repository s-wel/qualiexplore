Welcome to our website, where we showcase the power of conversational artificial intelligence (AI) in assisting companies with data quality management. This innovative approach is exemplified through two solutions developed as part of the EU-funded i4Q project (website).

The first solution is Audit Advisor, a chatbot specifically designed to support data quality audits. It provides guidance by leading users through a comprehensive questionnaire, enabling them to assess the effectiveness of their organization's data quality management, whether it pertains to the entire production process or a specific segment of it. For detailed information on the audit procedure, you can access the documentation on the i4Q project website.

The second solution is i4Q QualiExplore, a web-based knowledge base aimed at identifying relevant factors that influence data quality within production. It offers a user-friendly interface that allows users to explore and navigate through factor descriptions. Additionally, a complementary chatbot is available to enhance the user experience. For detailed information on QualiExplore, you can access the documentation on the i4Q project's website.

Explore the potential of conversational AI in data quality management through the Audit Advisor and QualiExplore solutions developed as part of the i4Q project.
